Package design

I participated in various package design projects. I set key colors to design based on the product's target audience and market. I designed an iron character to emphasize that it is a product for children. Despite numerous meetings throughout the final stages, the outcome was successful.

Branding - Velon

I've branded numerous new products, but this particular one holds a special significance for me. It's mainly targeted at individuals dealing with venous-related issues. Therefore, I opted for bright colors, natural shapes, and curved lines. Additionally, since the East Asia market (Dubai, Qatar) prefers the color purple, I utilized it as the primary color.

Branding - Ferosom

This project involved adding a new product to an existing product line. Before getting started, I conducted research to examine people's preferences for design styles and websites through online search and meeting with Marketing manager and Art director. Additionally, I designed the project within the boundaries of the existing branding, ensuring that it didn't deviate significantly. I was responsible for the brand logo, website design, leaflets, and online promotional materials for the high-dosage product and kids' gummy products.

Website design

I had the responsibility of designing and developing approximately 10 websites, which include both the company's main website and various product-specific sites. My approach involved tailoring each website to prominently showcase the distinctive features of the products.
